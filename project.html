About Us

Welcome to the Alumni Portal of Business School, a dynamic platform designed to unite and empower our global community of alumni. Built on the principles of collaboration, innovation, and lifelong learning, our portal offers an array of features tailored to enhance your alumni experience.

Connect: Join a vibrant network of alumni spanning diverse industries, geographies, and generations. Engage with fellow graduates through discussion forums, interest groups, and networking events.

Discover: Access exclusive resources, articles, and webinars curated to support your personal and professional development. Stay informed about industry trends, job opportunities, and upcoming events relevant to your career.

Contribute: Give back to your alma mater and support the next generation of students. Mentor aspiring professionals, volunteer for alumni-led initiatives, and participate in fundraising campaigns to advance the mission of Business School.

New Features:

  • Alumni Directory: Easily search and connect with fellow alumni based on criteria such as graduation year, industry, or location.
  • Virtual Mentorship Program: Engage in one-on-one mentorship opportunities with experienced alumni mentors who can offer guidance and advice in your field of interest.
  • Job Board: Explore job openings posted by alumni-friendly employers and organizations seeking candidates with your skillset and experience.
  • Alumni Spotlight: Showcase the achievements and success stories of notable alumni through featured profiles and interviews, inspiring others within the community.
  • Interactive Events Calendar: Stay updated on upcoming alumni reunions, career fairs, and networking events with an interactive calendar feature that allows you to RSVP and add events to your schedule.

Join us on this journey of connection, growth, and impact. Together, let's harness the collective power of the Business School alumni network to create opportunities, foster innovation, and make a difference in the world.
